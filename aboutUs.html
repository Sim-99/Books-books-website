Get to Know Us

From a Single Bookshelf to Thousands of Libraries

Book's Books began in 2010 when our founder, Sarah Johnson, noticed the stark difference in library resources between schools in different communities. What started as a single bookshelf donation has grown into a movement.

Today, we've established over 500 libraries across 3 countries, donated 1 million+ books, and trained thousands of volunteers in literacy advocacy.

Our Commitment to Literacy

We believe every child deserves access to books and the opportunity to discover the magic of reading. Our mission is threefold:

  • Access: Provide books to underserved communities
  • Education: Train educators and volunteers in literacy programs
  • Inspiration: Create spaces that inspire lifelong reading habits

Our Vision

A world where every child has access to quality reading materials and the support to develop strong literacy skills.

Inclusivity

We serve all communities regardless of background, ensuring every child sees themselves in the stories we provide.

Community Engagement

Literacy Advocacy

Sustainability
